Sequelize Association Helper Methods

In Sequelize, when associations are defined between models using methods like hasMany(), belongsTo(), hasOne(), or belongsToMany(), Sequelize automatically generates special helper methods on model instances. These methods allow us to interact with related data in an object-oriented way instead of writing manual SQL queries.

Example:

City.hasMany(Airport);
Airport.belongsTo(City);

After defining this relationship, Sequelize automatically adds methods such as:

getAirports() → fetch all airports of a city
createAirport() → create a new airport linked to a city
addAirport() → associate an airport with a city
removeAirport() → remove association
countAirports() → count related airports
